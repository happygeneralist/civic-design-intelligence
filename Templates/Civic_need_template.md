# Civic need title

A civic need describes what the institution, public service or system must uphold for people, communities or public outcomes.

Use civic needs when the research points to institutional obligations, public value, rights, fairness, accountability, access burden, redressability, system-level failure or conditions needed for trust, dignity, agency or equitable service access.

During the MVP, civic needs may live in `003_User_needs/` using the `CN_000` ID pattern. A later schema review can decide whether they need a separate folder.

## Working formulation

Write the civic need clearly and avoid turning it into a solution requirement too early.

```text
The service/system/institution needs to [uphold/enable/protect] [condition/public value] so that [public outcome or civic consequence].
```

Do not force this structure if it distorts the meaning.
