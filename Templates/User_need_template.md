# Parents need clear, trustworthy guidance to plan SEND university transitions

### Summary
Parents want accessible, reliable information about SEND provision at universities so they can make confident decisions. They feel overwhelmed by fragmented and inconsistent sources, and want a trusted way to compare support options.

### Value and impact
- **Perceived value:** Enables parents to make confident, informed decisions about SEND transitions.  
- **Civic value:** Aligns with the obligation to provide equitable access to education.  
- **Measurement:** Satisfaction and confidence scores in transition-planning surveys.  
- **Decision rationale:** Prioritised for early release due to high emotional and civic burden.
### Evidence of success
- Parents describe spending hours searching online and cross-checking information.
- Frequent mention of confusion around eligibility and accommodations.
- Emotional tone: anxious, uncertain, exhausted.

**Expected outcomes**
- Increased confidence and autonomy in decision-making.
- Reduced stress for families during transition planning.
