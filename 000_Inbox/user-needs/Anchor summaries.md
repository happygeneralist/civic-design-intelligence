## **Young People (YP) with SEND – Needs**

> These have been summarised with the help of [Gov User Needs LLM](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-673f960b95b48191802b8036a6bdc3a8-gov-user-needs) and should be **handled as assumptions until fully anchored to the research**.

### Identity & Confidence

- I need to feel like I have a future.
- I need to believe I am capable and valued.
- I need others to see my potential, not just my challenge s.
- I need to know that I can work or contribute meaningfully.
- I need to be supported to explore what I’m good at.
- I need to feel safe and confident to try new things.
- I need to recover my aspirations after setbacks.

### Navigation & Decision-Making

- I need help choosing between different options.
- I need to see what different paths could look like for someone like me.
- I need to take things one step at a time, not all at once.
- I need support to make informed decisions when I’m ready.
- I need to understand how my interests connect to careers.
- I need to know what qualifications I need for my goals.
- I need time and space to figure things out at my pace.

### Mental Health & Emotional Wellbeing

- I need support for my mental health before I can think about the future.
- I need help when I feel overwhelmed or shut down.
- I need to feel like I’m not alone or failing.
- I need therapeutic or clinical support when I’m struggling.
- I need people to understand that my anxiety or withdrawal isn’t laziness.

### Access to Information

- I need information that is clear, visual, and not overwhelming.    
- I need information in formats I can engage with (e.g. short videos, games, apps).
- I need to know what’s realistic and achievable for me.
- I need to find everything in one place — not scattered across different services.
- I need to know what’s available **after age 16** and **after 19**.
- I need tools that help me keep track of my goals and progress.
- I need examples of people like me succeeding.

### Support & Relationships

- I need people I trust to help me explore my options.    
- I need careers advice that is tailored to me.
- I need support from people who understand SEND.
- I need someone who can guide me through the system.
- I need people to give me a chance — socially, in school, and at work.

## Parents & Carers – Needs

### Planning & Decision Support

- I need to know what options are out there now and in the future.
- I need help developing a plan and a plan B for my child.
- I need to understand what’s available after 16 and after 19.
- I need to make decisions when my child can’t — and feel confident they’re right.
- I need someone to help me interpret and explain information to my child.
  

### Information & Navigation

- I need one place to find reliable information.
- I need information that’s simple, visual, and not contradictory.
- I need to understand eligibility, deadlines, and processes.
- I need to know what support exists for different levels of need.
- I need to stop relying on luck, chance, or other parents to find what I need.

### Recognition & Emotional Support

- I need the system to stop being a fight at every stage.
- I need professionals to stop underestimating my child’s potential.
- I need to not feel invisible to schools or the local authority.
- I need space to recover from all the battles we’ve had.
- I need help carrying the emotional and decision-making burden.
    

### Inclusion & Access

- I need services that accommodate my child’s specific needs.
- I need employers, schools, and activities to give my child a chance.
- I need flexible and person-centred support that reflects who my child is.
- I need therapy and interventions to be available when needed.

---

## Cross-Cutting Needs (YP + Parents)

- We need joined-up, consistent support across education, health, and work.
- We need services and systems that don’t drop off at key transitions.
- We need a non-judgemental, inclusive approach from professionals.
- We need to stop being told to search for services without guidance.
- We need options to be visible, understandable, and tailored.
