Current status

Now

The repository has moved from structural setup into cautious research ingestion. The first bounded primary-research ingestion slice has been merged and is paused for team review before more research objects are added.

This public page is the current non-dependent work: making progress, direction and open questions easier to communicate without changing research interpretation.

What has been packaged so far

These are progress chunks, not finished product releases. The work is still an MVP, and research objects remain governed by status, evidence and review metadata.

16 June 2026

  • Clarified that filenames should start with stable IDs and may include readable slugs.

16 June 2026

Roadmap focus

1. Review the ingestion slice

Use team feedback to check whether the new parent/carer navigation-burden objects are framed at the right level and linked to the right evidence.

2. Continue bounded ingestion

Select the next small source or extract only after the team has reflected on the first slice and any template or wording issues it reveals.

3. Keep communicating progress

Use this page as the public-facing summary of what has changed, while keeping GitHub and object changelogs as the detailed audit trail.

What is intentionally not next

The project is avoiding heavier structure until worked examples show what is genuinely useful.

No broad migration

Further filename, link or metadata migrations should happen only when validation, Obsidian use or research review reveals a concrete problem.

No premature validation

Research objects should not be marked reviewed or validated until human review has happened and the evidence basis supports it.

No heavy platform layer

API, database, sync and automation work should wait until the Markdown-first workflow has been tested with more real examples.
